Urban Tree Coverage by Community Council Areas in Toronto

North York Has the Most Urban Trees in Toronto

North York has the most urban trees (195,064 trees) among the four community council areas in the city of Toronto; while Scarborough has the least urban trees (128,299 trees).


Maple is the most common urban tree found in Toronto

The most common type of urban tree in Toronto is Maple, which accounts for almost 30% in each community council area. North York is the area which has the most Maple trees (53,416 trees), but the area has the lowest percentage of Maple trees (27.38%) among the four community council areas.
